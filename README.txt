Left CTRL:	Charge the ball
SPACE:		Bounce
LEFT/RIGHT: Go left or right

If the threshold indicated by the marker on the charge bar
has been reached, the ball will automatically ignite all
platforms it falls upon and bounce!

The goal is to try to go as far as possible while ingiting
the maximum number of platforms

Once the lives are consumed the game will automatically end.
It will display the your score and automatically termiate
after 6 seconds
